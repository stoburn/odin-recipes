Lasagna

Tasty tasty lasagna!

This is a lasagna. This isn't your Garfield's lasagna. This is piping hot lava lasagna.

    Ingredients
  • 3 Cups Cheetos
  • 1 Cup Cheez-Whiz
  • 1 lb Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 Cup Ketchup
    Instructions
  1. Bring pot of water to a boil
  2. Put in ingredients
  3. Stir until sludgy
  4. Despair
  5. Go buy Stouffer's lasagna and nuke it.
  6. Place both lasagna adjacent to each other
  7. It's a race!
  8. Eat whichever lasagna wins
